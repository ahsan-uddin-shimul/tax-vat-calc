Detail calculation

- Please fill "Qty", "Unit" and "Rate" fields to automatically populate "Invoice Amount" field.

- Alternativel, you can only fill the "Invoice Amount" field to calculate directly. This will emptied out "Qty" and "Rate" field instantly.

- Select "Including Tax" and/or "Including Vat" checkbox as per the requirement along with respective rates in percent.

- Press "Enter" or "Submit" button to initiate the calculation.

- Once submitted the right side panel will populate to show detail calculation and breakdown.

- To emptied out all of the fields press "Reset" button, press "Logout" to exit the application.

- This is a web base application and multiple user can access the same platform from anywhere even from mobile devices.
